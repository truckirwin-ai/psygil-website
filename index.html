How it works

A structured pipeline for the work you already do

Psygil mirrors the way a careful evaluation actually flows, forensic or clinical, from referral to signed report. Each stage is enforced in order. The Writer cannot run before the clinician has confirmed a diagnosis. There is no override.

01 Intake Patient Record Gate Every identifying fact, court detail, and history element must be typed by the clinician before the case can proceed. No guessed fields.
02 Testing Validity Gate Every instrument administered and every score is recorded with the scorer, time, and result before the case moves to interview.
03 Interviews Interview Gate Structured mental status exam, behavioral observations, and defendant statements are captured in templates before diagnostic review opens.
04 Diagnostics Primary Gate The clinician must render, defer, or reject each proposed diagnosis, with signed reasoning, before the Writer agent can draft a single line.
05 Reports Editing & Legal Gate Editor review, clinician attestation, and cryptographic signature lock the report and seal the audit log for court discovery.

The gate at Diagnostics is the product. Nothing downstream runs without a signed clinical decision.

Why it matters

Evidence engine, not a decision engine

Collect facts and information, then assemble your diagnostics.

You decide, always

You are always in charge of every diagnostic decision. The Writer agent cannot draft a single line until you have rendered, deferred, or rejected each diagnosis yourself.

Structured intake

The case begins with a structured and robust client onboarding. Identity, court information, charges, referral question, legal and family history. Every field typed by you, timestamped, and discoverable.

Testing and scoring

You select the appropriate measure. Psygil collates the scores and data, tracks who scored what and when, and preserves the full record for discovery.

Interview insights

Automatically summarize and organize interview information and insights. Every observation tied to date and context, every quote traceable back to source.

Diagnostic assembly

The Diagnostician tool pulls the important data elements from intake, testing, and interviews into a single evidence map. DSM-5-TR criteria, supporting quotes, source citations. You render the diagnosis.

Signed and defensible

Every signed report carries a cryptographic attestation tied to your identity. When opposing counsel issues discovery, you can reproduce every observation, every quote, every decision, in minutes.

Built for

Clinicians who write reports that hold up under scrutiny

Forensic psychologists

Competency, sanity, risk, and sentencing evaluators who carry a personal case list and need a defensible record from referral to testimony. Cross-examination becomes a matter of reading the record, not reconstructing it.

Clinical evaluators

Psychologists performing IMEs, disability assessments, capacity evaluations, fitness-for-duty, and diagnostic consults where the referral source expects a rigorous, auditable report. Same pipeline, same attestation.

Group practices

Forensic and clinical practices that share intake templates, test batteries, and report style across multiple clinicians. Central workspace, per-clinician audit trail, shared style guide.
