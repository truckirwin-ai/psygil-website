How purchase works

From click to installer in one business day

Card payment and recurring billing go through Stripe. Fulfillment is manual so we know who you are and can answer questions before you deploy. The full flow in three steps.

01

Start by card, through Stripe

Click Start Solo or Start Practice. Stripe handles the card transaction, sets up the monthly subscription, and emails you a receipt immediately. Psygil never sees the card number.

02

We confirm, you get the key

Within one business day, you receive an email from support@psygil.com with your license key, download links for macOS, Windows, and Linux, and a short activation guide.

03

Install and activate

Run the installer for your platform. Paste the license key on first launch. Each seat activates on one workstation and can be reassigned later from your account page. The key stays active as long as your subscription is current.

Questions before you buy? Write to sales@psygil.com. Enterprise and BAA inquiries go to the same address.

What every subscription includes

The entire pipeline, on your hardware

Works offline

The PII detection sidecar runs locally. If you disconnect the laptop from the network, intake, testing, interviews, and audit all keep working. Only remote AI inference requires network, and PII is redacted before any remote call.

Your data, your disk

Case files live in a workspace folder on the clinician's machine. Nothing is stored on a Psygil server because there is no Psygil server for case data. The license server only verifies license keys.

Bring your own model key

Psygil uses the clinician's own Anthropic, OpenAI, or Azure OpenAI key for the Writer, Editor, Ingestor, and Diagnostician agents. API costs are billed to the clinician by the model provider, not by Psygil.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this a subscription?

Yes. Solo and Practice are billed monthly. Enterprise is a monthly subscription with a one-time setup fee at the start of the engagement. Every tier includes software updates for the supported major version, local PII detection, and email support for as long as the subscription is current. Cancel any time and your access continues through the end of the current billing period.

What happens after I pay?

Stripe emails you a receipt immediately and schedules the next month's charge on the same day of the month. Within one business day, support@psygil.com sends your license key, download links for macOS, Windows, and Linux installers, and a short activation guide. Activate the app with your key on first launch. Each seat can be activated on one workstation at a time and reassigned if needed.

Which payment methods work?

All major credit and debit cards through Stripe. For ACH, wire transfer, purchase orders, or non-card payment, write to sales@psygil.com and we will send an invoice.

Does Psygil store my case files?

No. Case files are stored in a workspace folder on your machine. The Psygil license server verifies keys and does not receive or store any case data. PII detection runs locally in a sidecar process.

What about AI API costs?

The AI agents use your own Anthropic, OpenAI, or Azure OpenAI API key. You pay the model provider directly for inference. A typical full evaluation runs well under one dollar in API charges depending on the provider and model you select.

Can I try it before buying?

Write to sales@psygil.com with a short note about your practice. We offer a time-limited evaluation license for qualified forensic and clinical psychologists, and for referring counsel.

HIPAA and BAAs

Psygil is a desktop tool. Case data stays on the clinician's device. HIPAA obligations fall on the covered entity or business associate operating the device. For enterprise deployments that require a BAA with Foundry SMB, contact sales. Your model provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Azure) is a separate BAA relationship you manage with them.

Cancellations and refunds

All sales are final. Psygil does not issue refunds, prorated credits, or partial reimbursements for any monthly payment once it has been processed, and the Enterprise setup fee is non-refundable once work has started.

You may cancel at any time from your account page or by writing to support@psygil.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid period. You keep full access to the software, support, and included updates through the last day of that period, and you are not billed again. If you start a Solo subscription on April 14 and cancel on April 20, your license remains active until May 14, then lapses. The same rule applies to every tier.

If the software does not work as described on a supported platform, write to support@psygil.com within 14 days of your first charge and we will troubleshoot. If we cannot resolve the defect, we will issue a refund for the most recent monthly charge at our discretion. This exception does not apply to changes in your workflow, hardware, or operating system after purchase.

Which operating systems are supported?

macOS 12 or later (Apple Silicon and Intel), Windows 10 and 11, and most modern Linux distributions with glibc 2.31 or newer. See the download page for specific installers.
