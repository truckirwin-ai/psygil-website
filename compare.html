Where others lead

Honest tradeoffs build trust.

  • Psynth leads on assessment breadth. If you need instrument-count parity above forensic depth, Psynth is a reasonable choice.
  • PsychAssist leads on broad forensic marketing. Their claims are broad; Psygil's answer is architectural proof.
  • PsychReport.ai leads on entry-tier pricing. If cost alone wins, they win. If defensibility matters, Psygil wins.
  • FT Nexus leads on offender normative databases. Psygil's future roadmap addresses this, pending licensing.
  • PAR leads on proprietary assessment data integration. PARiConnect is its own assessment ecosystem.
  • ReportQuill leads on deterministic-template messaging. Psygil adds signed diagnostic gates, attestation, and audit proof.
