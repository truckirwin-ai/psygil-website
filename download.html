How it works

Three steps from purchase to case one

STEP 01

Pick a tier and write us

Choose Solo, Practice, or Enterprise on the pricing page. The button opens an email to sales@psygil.com with the tier pre-filled. Reply with the name and email address the license should be registered to.

STEP 02

Receive your license email

The same business day, you receive an email with your license key and signed download links for macOS, Windows, and Linux. Keep the email. Your key is the only thing that activates the app.

STEP 03

Install and activate

Download the installer for your operating system. Launch Psygil. Paste your license key at first run. The app verifies the key once, then runs offline for the life of the license. Open your first case.

Enterprise buyers with procurement requirements: write to sales@psygil.com and we will route you to a contract-ready signer and a BAA if required.

Supported platforms

Native installers for the three operating systems clinicians actually use

macOS

12 Monterey or later

Universal installer for Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4) and Intel. Signed and notarized. Gatekeeper approved. Installs to /Applications.

Signed MSI installer. Runs without administrator rights after the initial install. Integrates with the Windows credential store for license key persistence.

Before you install

Answers to the questions clinicians ask first

Do I need an internet connection?

Only to activate the license and to call the remote AI model provider for the Writer, Editor, Ingestor, and Diagnostician agents. Intake, testing, interviews, PII detection, and the audit log run without network. If you disconnect during case work, the app keeps working and queues nothing to a remote server because there is no Psygil server for case data.

Do I need my own model API key?

Yes. Psygil uses your Anthropic, OpenAI, or Azure OpenAI key. You paste it into the app on first configuration, and the app stores it in your operating system's secure credential store. API costs go directly to the model provider, not to Psygil.

Where are case files stored?

In a workspace folder on your machine. You pick the folder during setup. Local disk, an encrypted volume, or a network share you control. Psygil never copies case files off your device.

What about updates?

Minor updates and bug fixes are free for the life of your license. Major version upgrades are offered as a separate upgrade purchase at a discount for existing license holders. You are never forced to update. Your purchased version keeps running.

I lost my email. How do I recover my license key?

Write to support@psygil.com from the email address on the original purchase. We verify the account and reissue the key and download links.
